We have created this website as a resource to empower individuals to understand the relationship between computer science and human bias. We strive to bring power and humanity back into our technology. Farewell "ghosts" in the machine.

This song expresses the idea of decreasing humanity with increasing technological reliance. The sound is infused with classic R&B touches, modern techno beats, and has a moody, otherworldly feel to it. This perfectly encapsulates how I feel while learning about the relationship between code and power in the Library and Information Science course we've created this page for.
