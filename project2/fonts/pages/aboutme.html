Hi! I come from a Mexican-American family, a home buzzing with driven, and creative individuals. I am currently a student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. My project partner Kyra and I have created this website as a resource to empower individuals to understand the relationship between computer science and human bias. My professional direction fuses research experience from the Social and Moral Learning Lab with a growing focus on community empowerment, advocacy, developmental psychology, research, sustainability, and technology. My academic path, including study abroad coursework in culture, history and sustainability, reflect a profound interest in how psychology research can provide innovative changes in art, culture, and technology. At the convergence of human rights and environmental equity, I bring a unique cognitive-behavioral perspective to the improvement of our communities.
